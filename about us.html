SANA SAFINAS

Sana Safinaz is a renowned luxury fashion brand from Pakistan, established in 1989 by designers Sana Hashwani and Safinaz Muneer. Known for its elegant and contemporary designs, the brand offers ready-to-wear collections, including formal, semi-formal, and casual wear. Sana Safinaz is celebrated for its intricate embroidery, high-quality fabrics, and sophisticated color palettes. The brand has gained international recognition for blending traditional craftsmanship with modern styles. It also has a strong presence in bridal couture, offering exquisite and luxurious wedding collections. Sana Safinaz continues to be a leading name in the global fashion industry..

ELAN

Élan is a prestigious Pakistani fashion brand founded by designer Khadijah Shah in 2006. Known for its luxurious and contemporary designs, Élan offers a wide range of collections, including prêt-à-porter, haute couture, and bridal wear. The brand is renowned for its opulent fabrics, intricate embellishments, and sophisticated embroidery, blending traditional artistry with modern aesthetics. Élan’s bridal collections are particularly celebrated for their grandeur and intricate detailing, making them a popular choice for weddings. With a focus on elegance and craftsmanship, Élan has become a symbol of high-end fashion in Pakistan and internationally. The brand also embraces a blend of classic and contemporary styles, catering to a diverse clientele.

HSY

HSY (Hassan Shehryar Yasin) is a renowned Pakistani fashion designer and the founder of the HSY boutique, established in 2000. Known for its luxurious and opulent designs, HSY specializes in bridal couture, formal wear, and prêt-à-porter collections. The brand is celebrated for its exquisite craftsmanship, intricate embroidery, and use of rich fabrics, often incorporating traditional Pakistani techniques with modern, global trends. HSY's bridal collections are particularly iconic, featuring elaborate embellishments and timeless elegance. With a strong presence in both Pakistan and internationally, HSY continues to be a symbol of glamour, sophistication, and high fashion in the industry.

Zara Shahjahan Botique

Zara Shahjahan is a celebrated Pakistani fashion brand founded by designer Zara Shahjahan in 2004. Known for its distinct blend of traditional and contemporary designs, the boutique offers a range of collections, including ready-to-wear, bridal couture, and luxury pret. Zara Shahjahan is recognized for its intricate craftsmanship, delicate hand-embroideries, and use of luxurious fabrics. The brand often incorporates vintage-inspired styles with modern twists, creating timeless and elegant pieces. Zara Shahjahan's bridal collections are particularly popular, known for their opulent designs and attention to detail. The boutique's designs have a signature classic yet youthful aesthetic, making it a sought-after name in the Pakistani fashion industry.

Maheen Khan Botique

Maheen Khan is a pioneering fashion designer and the founder of Maheen Khan Boutique, one of Pakistan's most respected fashion labels. Established in the 1970s, Maheen Khan is often referred to as one of the first designers to introduce high-end prêt-à-porter fashion in Pakistan. The boutique is known for its elegant and timeless designs, blending traditional Pakistani craftsmanship with modern and minimalist aesthetics. Maheen Khan's collections often feature fine fabrics, hand-crafted details, and a focus on wearable luxury. Her bridal wear is particularly renowned for its refined elegance and intricate embroidery. As a fashion icon, Maheen Khan continues to influence the industry, contributing to the evolution of Pakistani fashion with her creative vision.
