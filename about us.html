SANA SAFINAS

Design Aesthetic: Sana Safinaz is celebrated for its sophisticated, modern, and chic style. The brand combines traditional craftsmanship with contemporary trends, often blending intricate embroidery, fine fabrics, and luxurious detailing. The collections range from casual wear to formal wear, with a strong emphasis on elegance and femininity. Fabric and Quality: The brand is known for using high-quality fabrics such as silk, chiffon, lawn, and cotton. They are also recognized for their use of intricate embroideries and embellishments, which give their clothing a luxurious and refined look. Collections: Sana Safinaz offers a wide range of collections, including bridal wear, formal wear, semi-formal wear, and casual outfits. They also have a popular line of luxury pret (ready-to-wear), which is known for its stylish and comfortable yet elegant designs.

ELAN

Design Aesthetic: Élan’s style is celebrated for its fusion of intricate traditional craftsmanship with modern, luxurious cuts and silhouettes. The brand is known for its regal, feminine, and contemporary designs, often incorporating rich embroidery, embellishments, and innovative textures. Élan's creations exude a sense of timelessness, elegance, and opulence, making them ideal for high-profile events, weddings, and formal occasions. Bridal Collections: Élan is particularly renowned for its bridal wear, offering exquisite wedding dresses that feature elaborate embroidery, beadwork, and luxurious fabrics. Their bridal collections are characterized by lavish designs, intricate detailing, and a focus on creating statement-making, dream-like gowns for brides. Fabrics and Craftsmanship: The brand uses high-quality materials such as silk, chiffon, velvet, georgette, and organza. Élan’s signature craftsmanship includes intricate handwork, such as thread embroidery, sequins, beads, and crystals, which enhance the luxurious appeal of their garments.
